About Me

Hi, I’m Amina, I graduated in Business Administration in 2019 and I’m currently studying Full-Stack Web Development to expand my digital and technical skills.

I’m learning to build web applications from the ground up using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, as well as backend technologies and modern frameworks. Alongside this, I explore how AI tools can assist and speed up creative and technical tasks — but I’m committed to understanding what happens “under the hood”.

I enjoy structured thinking, visual clarity, and purposeful design. I believe in combining technical knowledge with soft skills like communication, adaptability, and curiosity — and that’s exactly the direction I’m growing in.

Right now I’m looking for remote or hybrid opportunities — internships, assistant roles or collaborative projects — where I can contribute, learn and keep building real experience.

Feel free to reach out if you’d like to connect!
